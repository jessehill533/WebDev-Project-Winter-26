About This Page

This page is about Dave Matthews and includes a snippet about his background/skills, a graph demonstrating his band's live performance ability, and a video of John Mayer performing with him in one of the most powerful and moving live collaborations of all time.

Interesting details about Dave Matthews

  1. Background
    • Born in South Africa (his music was heavily inspired by South African culture)
    • Moved to Charlottesville Virginia in 1986
    • Was a bartender at "Millers"
    • Was originally doubted by his soon-to-be bandmates because of his extremely unique style
    • Guitar technique was heavily influenced by the unconventional finger-spreading, chordal style of King Crimson's Robert Fripp
  2. Accomplishments
    • Founded the Dave Matthews Band in 1991, which became one of the most successful touring bands in history.
    • Achieved multiple chart-topping albums, including Crash and Before These Crowded Streets, with millions of records sold worldwide.
    • Won a Grammy Award for Best Rock Vocal Performance for the song “So Much to Say” in 1997.
    • Known for record-breaking live performances and consistently ranking among the highest-grossing touring artists globally.
    • Recognized for philanthropic efforts, including environmental activism and charitable work through the Bama Works Fund.
  3. Interesting Facts
    • He has appeared in several films and TV shows, including Because of Winn-Dixie and House.
    • He is a private pilot and has a strong interest in flying.
    • He has a passion for winemaking and co-owns a winery in Virginia.
    • He wrote many early songs in just a few months before forming his band, despite having little formal musical training.
    • He is known for collaborating with a wide range of artists across genres, from rock to jazz to country.
